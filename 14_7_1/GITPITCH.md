### 14.7.1 Hands with Four-of-a-kind

`$ \{A\clubsuit,2\clubsuit,2\heartsuit,2\diamondsuit,2\spadesuit\} $`

`$ (2,A,\clubsuit) \leftrightarrow \{2\clubsuit,2\heartsuit,2\diamondsuit,2\spadesuit,A\clubsuit \} $`
